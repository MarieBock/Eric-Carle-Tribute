Author and Illustrator

Photo of Eric Carle

Eric Carle is well-known for the Very Hungry Caterpillar book. It was the first book that was published and illustrated by him. Since then there have been many more colorful books.

Some of the other books include:

  • 1968 - 1, 2, 3 to the Zoo
  • 1970 - The Tiny Seed
  • 1971 - Do You Want To Be My Friend?
  • 1972 - The Secret Birthday Message
  • 1972 - Rooster's Off To See The World
  • 1973 - Have You Seen My Cat?
  • 1973 - I See a Song
  • 1975 - The Mixed-Up Chameleon
  • 1975 - Pancakes, Pancakes
  • 1977 - The Grouchy Ladybug
  • 1980 - The Very Quiet Cricket
  • 1984 - The Very Busy Spider
  • 1986 - Papa, Please Get The Moon For Me
  • 1987 - A House For Hermit Crab
  • 1991 - Polar Bear, Polar Bear, What Do You Hear?
  • 1993 - Today Is Monday
  • 1995 - The Very Lonely Firefly
  • 1996 - Little Cloud
  • 2000 - Dream Snow
  • 2000 - Does a Kangaroo Have a Mother
  • 2002 - "Slowly, Slowly, Slowly, Slowly," Said The Sloth
  • 2004 - Mister Seahorse
  • 2005 - 10 Little Rubber Ducks
  • 2006 - Panda Bear, Panda Bear, What Do You See?
  • 2006 - My Very First Library
  • 2015 - The Nonsense Show
